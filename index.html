THE APPROACH OF OUR ASSOCIATION

At Aide et Action, we believe that it is the populations themselves who have the solutions to their problems. This is why we adopt an approach of listening and supporting local actors. Respecting the freedom and culture of these communities, our projects are led by Aide et Action employees and volunteers from the countries of intervention and are supported by local partner associations. The goal: to promote the autonomy of the populations and that of all the actors who revolve around them (local associations, parents of pupils, teachers, communities, etc.) We apply this principle of exchange and partnership within our structure: Aide et Action International is a network of international associations present in Asia, Africa, the Caribbean and Europe which pool their resources, ideas and skills.
